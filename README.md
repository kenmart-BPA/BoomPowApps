# BoomPowFit Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 7, 2026

BoomPowFit ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data BoomPowFit processes, how it is used, and your choices.

## Summary

- We do not sell your data.
- We do not use your data for advertising.
- We do not track you across apps or websites.
- Workout and plan data is used to provide app functionality.
- Health data access is optional and controlled by you in Apple Health permissions.

## Data We Process

BoomPowFit may process the following categories of data:

- Workout plans: plan names, exercise templates, target sets/reps/weights, notes.
- Workout logs: completed exercises, logged sets, reps, weights, timestamps, workout duration, plan linkage.
- Exercise catalog: built-in and custom exercises you add.
- Preferences: theme, units (lb/kg), and app behavior settings.
- Health data (optional): bodyweight read from Apple Health, and completed strength workout records written to Apple Health (if enabled by you).

## How We Use Data

We use data only to operate and improve your in-app experience, including:

- Creating and managing workout plans.
- Running today’s workout flow and logging completed sets.
- Showing trends and history (for example, volume load and bodyweight trend).
- Syncing your app data across your devices through iCloud/CloudKit when available.
- Exporting and importing your data when you request it.

## HealthKit Data

If you grant permission, BoomPowFit can:

- Read bodyweight from Apple Health to show bodyweight trend.
- Write completed strength workouts to Apple Health.

Health data is used only for in-app fitness functionality and is not used for advertising or sold. You can revoke Health access at any time in Apple Health settings.

## Storage and Sync

- Data is stored on-device.
- If iCloud/CloudKit is enabled for your account/device, data may sync across your devices signed into the same Apple ID.
- If iCloud is unavailable, BoomPowFit falls back to local on-device storage.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share personal data with data brokers or ad networks.

Data may be processed by Apple services you use (for example, iCloud/CloudKit and HealthKit) under Apple’s terms and privacy policies.

## Data Retention and Deletion

- Your data is retained until you delete it in the app, remove the app, or disable iCloud sync for the app.
- You can delete plans/workouts inside the app.
- You can remove Health permissions in Apple Health at any time.

## Security

We use platform-provided protections (Apple frameworks and device security controls). No method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

## Children’s Privacy

BoomPowFit is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

## International Users

Data processing may occur where Apple services operate when using iCloud/CloudKit.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the Effective date above when changes are made.
