BoomNote

Privacy Policy — Effective March 29, 2026

The short version

BoomNote collects nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. Everything stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. BoomNote does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Your note text, recipient email address, and subject line are saved only in your device’s local storage using the standard iOS preferences system. That data never leaves your device unless you choose to send a note using your own iOS Mail account.

How notes are sent

When you tap OK, BoomNote opens iOS Mail pre-addressed with your note. The email is sent by your own Mail account — BoomPowApps has no access to, and never sees, the content of your notes or who you send them to.

Third-party services

BoomNote uses no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or tracking frameworks of any kind.

No analytics No ads No crash reporting No tracking No accounts

Data stored on your device

The following is stored locally in iOS UserDefaults and is never transmitted:

Note text Recipient email address Email subject line App preferences

You can clear this data at any time by deleting the app.

Children’s privacy

BoomNote does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. We will not make material changes without updating that date.
